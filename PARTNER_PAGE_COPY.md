## FAQ SECTION

**Section Headline:** "Frequently Asked Questions"

**Q1: How much does it cost to become a partner?**
A: Joining Xpress Autozone is free. We operate on a commission basis—you only pay when you make a sale. Our competitive commission structure is transparent and designed to reward your success.

**Q2: What percentage commission do you take?**
A: Commission rates vary based on product category and vendor tier. On average, we take 10-15% per transaction. All fees are clearly outlined in your partnership agreement with no hidden charges.

**Q3: How quickly will I get paid?**
A: We process payouts every Monday for orders completed the previous week. Payments are transferred directly to your bank account or mobile money within 24-48 hours.

**Q4: Can I manage multiple product categories?**
A: Absolutely! You can list any auto parts products you carry—from engine components to accessories. Our dashboard supports unlimited product uploads and categories.

**Q5: What if a customer has an issue with their order?**
A: Our dispute resolution system handles customer issues fairly. We mediate between you and the customer to find a quick resolution, protecting both parties.

**Q6: Do you provide shipping/logistics?**
A: We have preferred logistics partners, but you can arrange your own delivery. We integrate with major courier services for seamless tracking and updates.

**Q7: How do I upload my products?**
A: Our bulk upload tool accepts CSV files, making it easy to add hundreds of products at once. Or upload individually through our user-friendly dashboard.

**Q8: Can I adjust my prices anytime?**
A: Yes! You have complete control over your pricing. Adjust prices anytime directly from your dashboard. Price changes take effect immediately.

**Q9: What happens if my product doesn't sell?**
A: No pressure! Your products stay listed indefinitely until you choose to remove them. We provide analytics and recommendations to help optimize underperforming listings.

**Q10: Is there a minimum order requirement?**
A: No minimum order requirements. Sell as much or as little as you want. Scale at your own pace.

**Q11: How do you verify vendors and products?**
A: We conduct business verification during onboarding and periodically inspect product quality. This protects customers and ensures all vendors meet our standards.

**Q12: Can I cancel my partnership anytime?**
A: Yes, partnerships are flexible. You can pause or cancel anytime. However, we hope our service will exceed your expectations!
